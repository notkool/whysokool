Tinder for sneakerheads

Find people with same fashion.

So yes, swipe right if you think a shoe is hot and left if it’s not. The app learns your taste and personal style so you can settle on a pair that’s right. HIVE is the newest of the many apps that borrow the left-right swipe UI.

It aggregates more than 50,000 products from e-commerce sites, and whenever you make a purchase through the app, the startup gets a kickback. You can also keep your favorites on a wishlist, and the app alerts you when it goes on sale.
